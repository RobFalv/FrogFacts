The American Green Tree Frog

Photos by The Australian Musueum FrogID team, posted february 4, 2021 Source

Fact One: Green tree frogs are sometimes called rain frogs. Some people think that they are good indicators of rainy weather because they call loudest during damp weather.

Fact Two: Sometimes Green Tree Frogs are not even green! They can be brown and gray, and even have yellow and white stripes!

Sources:Fact One, Fact Two

The American Bullfrog

Photo by Nina Walk: posted June 18, 2019 Source
Second photo by John White. Source

Fact One: Bullfrogs are the largest species of frog in the U.S, and can grow up to eight inches long and weigh over a pound!

Fact Two: American Bullfrogs can jump around 2 meters(6 feet) with ease!

Sources:Fact One, Fact Two

The Poison Dart Frog

Photos by the San Diego Zoo: Source

Fact One: Poison Dart Frogs are just about the same size as a paper clip being 1 inch in size!

Fact Two: Poison Dart Frogs are some of the most poisonous animals on earth!

Sources:Fact One, Fact Two
